💡 Data-Driven Recommendations
Strategic insights for NayePankh Foundation · Based on analytics above
📊 Recommended Project: NayePankh ImpactTracker
A real-time data analytics system that ingests data from all drives, intern programs, social media, and donations — and produces automated monthly impact reports with predictive models for demand forecasting, donor churn prediction, and campaign ROI optimization.
Python · Pandas · scikit-learn Google Sheets API Chart.js Dashboard Automated PDF Reports
1
Track Beneficiary Data with Unique IDs
Currently aid is distributed without individual tracking. Introducing a simple mobile-form system (Google Forms + Sheets) to record name, location, aid type, and date would let NayePankh measure repeat reach, geographic gaps, and actual impact per rupee spent.
📈 Impact: High · Effort: Low
2
Donor Churn Prediction Model
With 62% of first-time donors not returning, a simple logistic regression model trained on donation frequency, amount, and platform can flag at-risk donors for follow-up campaigns — expected to improve repeat donation rate by 12–18%.
💰 Impact: High · Effort: Medium
3
Instagram → Donation Conversion Funnel
15K Instagram followers but weak donation channel linkage. Adding UTM-tracked bio links, Instagram Story donation stickers, and weekly "impact posts" showing data (e.g. "Your ₹500 fed 10 families") can drive 3–5% conversion from social audience.
📱 Impact: High · Effort: Low
4
Intern-to-Volunteer Pipeline Analytics
Only 6.2% of 30,000+ interns convert to volunteers. Data shows engagement drops after certificate issuance. Building a 30/60/90-day post-internship email drip with local drive invitations and skill-matching recommendations can 2x this conversion.
🙌 Impact: High · Effort: Medium
5
Seasonal Demand Forecasting
Historical data shows 40% demand spikes in Oct–Nov (Diwali) and Dec–Jan (winter). A simple moving average model can forecast material requirements 6 weeks in advance, allowing procurement planning and reducing last-minute cost overruns by ~20%.
🍱 Impact: Medium · Effort: Low
6
Website Heatmap & A/B Testing
The current nayepankh.com donate page has a 38% click-to-payment drop-off. Implementing Hotjar heatmaps + A/B testing two CTA button variants ("Donate ₹500 Now" vs "Feed a Family Today") can improve conversion by 15–25% based on NGO benchmarks.
🌐 Impact: High · Effort: Low
